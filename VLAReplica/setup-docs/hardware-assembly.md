## Camera mount assembly

### Top D455 mount

1. Print two snap-hooks and one backplate.
2. Attach the snap-hooks to the backplate with M3 screws.
3. Screw the assembled mount onto the rear of the D455 with M4 screws.
4. Add rubber grip tape inside each hook.

### Wrist webcam mount

1. Print the wrist camera mount from the referenced STL.
2. Attach the wrist mount to the end-effector with an M3 screw and nut.
3. Secure the webcam and verify that the view is stable during arm motion.

## Light box assembly

1. Build the PVC frame.
2. Attach the diffuser sheet to the +z face.
3. Mount the LED panels in the documented positions.
4. Mount the front-view camera near the +z/+y corner.
5. Add the tarp and background sheet.
6. Clamp the overhanging sheet so the workspace stays flat.

## SO-101 arm mounting and AprilTag placement

1. Clamp the SO-101 follower arm to the table.
2. Align the base so the center is approximately 16.5 in from the -x face.
3. Place the 4 cm AprilTag on the right side of the base.
4. Ensure the tag is flat and aligned to the base edges.
