Troubleshooting & checklists

Back to project page

Common failure points

  • Camera mount wobble
  • AprilTag wrinkles or poor tag detection
  • Background sheet sagging or wrinkling
  • Wrong camera index in the calibration script
  • SO-101 calibration not saved in the expected folder
  • Running a policy before matching the setup overlay

Consolidated checklists

  • D455 mount is secure and does not wobble.
  • Rubber grip tape is installed inside the top camera hooks.
  • Light box is bright and the diffuser is taut.
  • Background sheet is flat in the camera view.
  • AprilTag is aligned and flat.
  • SO-101 calibration JSON is in the correct directory.
  • Camera overlays match the reference images.
  • Policy evaluation only begins after the live scene matches the overlay.
