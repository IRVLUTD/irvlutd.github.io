Overview

About

The MultiAxisGantrySystem is a 2-axis automated gantry system designed specifically for mining environments. Its primary purpose is to systematically clear miners and their equipment of debris and dust using directed compressed air, improving safety and reducing manual cleaning time on the job site.

The system coordinates three stepper motors (Motors A, B, and C) over Modbus RTU via RS485. Motors A and B drive the gantry along the primary travel axes, while Motor C controls the lateral sweep. A solenoid valve delivers timed compressed-air pulses at each grid position, triggered automatically by the drive's digital output signal.

The documentation covers all hardware and software aspects of the system, including wiring diagrams, register mappings, calibration procedures, firmware code, and full operating instructions.

Key Features

2-Axis Motion

Coordinated control of three independent motor axes for full grid coverage across the target area.

Automated Air Blast

Solenoid valve triggered at each grid position delivers a timed compressed-air pulse to clear dust and debris.

HMI Serial Control

Operated via a web-based HMI over serial. Supports calibration, homing, auto-step, manual positioning, and emergency stop.

Auto Calibration & Homing

Automatic limit-switch homing and travel-range calibration stored in EEPROM for reliable repeatable motion.

Configurable Grid

Grid dimensions (rows × columns) are configurable at runtime via HMI command, with snake-pattern auto-sweep.

Modbus RTU over RS485

All motor drives communicate on a shared RS485 bus using Modbus RTU at 38400 baud — robust and industrial-grade.
